Hotel Booking Analysis (2019–2023)

Power BI · Hospitality Project

Project Overview

Analyzed 4 years of hotel booking data (2019–2023) to uncover patterns in occupancy, revenue growth, customer demographics, cancellations, and seasonal demand trends. Designed an end-to-end analytics dashboard using Power BI, including data modeling, DAX measure creation, and visualization of hotel KPIs.

Key Visuals in Dashboard

  • Occupancy Heatmap across room types & seasons
  • Monthly Revenue Trends (YoY)
  • Room Type Performance & ADR comparison
  • Booking Lead Time Distribution
  • Customer Demographics & Segmentation
  • Cancellation Trends & Root Causes

Analytics Performed

  • Revenue forecasting using time-series patterns
  • Seasonality analysis for pricing optimization
  • Cancellation behavior modeling
  • Channel profitability comparison (OTA, Direct, Travel Agents)
  • Room allocation efficiency insights

What I Did

  • Cleaned, merged, and validated multi-year booking datasets
  • Designed a star-schema data model (FactBookings + Dimension tables)
  • Created DAX measures: Occupancy %, RevPAR, ADR, Lead Time, Cancellations, Forecasting
  • Designed interactive visuals with drill-downs for monthly, customer & channel insights
  • Built a business-ready dashboard for revenue & operations teams
